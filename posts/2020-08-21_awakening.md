 Today I stand awakened   
 Asking questions that I seldom asked   
 Who am I? Why am I alive? What is my purpose?   
   
 Feeling a deep sense of detachment   
 To everything surrounding me including you   
 Letting go of every desire   
 Not letting it define me anymore   
 Removing every layer like shriveled fabric   
 To become completely transparent   
   
 Accepting the new found inner peace   
 Life is what it is   
 Never better, never worse   
 Allowing the universe to conspire   
 to make something beautiful happen   
   
 Slowly losing all fear   
 For fear is nothing but fear of death   
 living fully in the present   
 Not worrying about the future   
 Nor regretting the past   
   
 Finding my way to the eternal state of happiness   
 Not by rearranging the circumstances of my life   
 But by realizing myself at the deepest level   
 and oneness with the universe
