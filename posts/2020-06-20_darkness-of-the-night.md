 The day scampers quickly   
 giving way to the darkness of the night   
 your many memories crowd tightly   
 teasing me, to haunt and fight   
   
 Together we walked hand in hand   
 Lost in our self, insulated from the world   
 Walking out, you left me alone to stand   
 Unabashedly I cry with no hand to hold   
   
 The unexpected rain on a hot day   
 Drenches me with the sudden downpour   
 Bringing a smile from a bygone day   
 Creating a false hope, you will return to the fore   
   
 The huge distance between us   
 shaped by years of bitterness   
 Engulfed with rage, fault and blame   
 Left us unable to embrace the light of love   
 In the absolute darkness of the night
