A new beginning,  
a new thought—  
a chance to touch the life  
we once dreamed of,  
even if only in distant memory.

Back then,  
we were naked strangers  
to everything but hope  
Life was tender,  
our dreams still untested,  
our memories soft  
and uncorrupted

And sometimes,  
I wonder what it would mean  
to start from that quiet place again—  
not rewriting the past,  
but meeting it with the person  
I’ve grown into

To walk a quieter path,  
mindful of the missteps  
that once broke us open  
To release the sharpness,  
the anger,  
the tangled things  
we carried without knowing,  
fighting our own ideas  
of right and wrong

But I’m older now,  
and gentler with myself  
I know any new road  
has its shadows,  
its turns I can’t predict,  
its storms that could return me  
to the same crossroads  
years from today

It was never the path,  
was it?  
It was who we were  
walking it with…

And now—  
I’m ready for a love  
that walks beside me  
without fear  
Someone who holds my hand  
when the world feels heavy,  
and lets me lean  
when I can’t stand steady  
Someone I can cry with  
and still feel whole  
Someone…

Maybe that’s you  
Maybe it isn’t  
But the beginning I seek  
lives in me now—  
quiet, warm,  
open to what may come

A new beginning  
starts today  
And I walk toward it,  
hugging the shadow  
standing beside me…
