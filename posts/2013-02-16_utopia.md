  Take me away, far away  
  I'm tired, tired, really tired to bow  
  Complete sadness has engulfed my world  
  Leaving nothing but black darkness glow  
  Take me away, far away  
    
  Tears stopped pouring long ago  
  Cause they are tired of raining on the go  
  My voice has gone subdued despite mellow  
  For it cannot find any solace in red or indigo  
  Take me away, far away  
    
  Surrounded by false hopes, my world  
  Even the star twinkling is a mirage somehow  
  The clouds, the sun, the belongings  
  Everything laughs as it walks in tow  
  Take me away, far away  
    
  I want to run from myself  
  Run from my very own shadow  
  Nothing about me is right  
  As my life is tethered to insanity cargo  
  Take me away, far away  
    
  Let me go to sleep, a deep sleep  
  All those demons, stop pestering me, forego   
  Take me away, to an utopian world   
  To live a life that is not built on ego  
  Take me now, far away  
  Take me away, far away
