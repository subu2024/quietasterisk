excerpt: "A poem of missed opportunities in love where hesitation leaves the narrator haunted by the words 'if only'."
---

With shaking arms and a pounding heart  
I walked to her, slowly,  
To open up and tell her what I felt  
But lo! I stopped as I saw her  
Laughing in the arms of another  
Turning quietly, I walked away  
Thinking to myself .. if only ..

One fine day, she called me  
A girl from Texas, sweet and cheerful  
Talked to me like she knew me long  
Setting my heart pining for her  
Until one day, she said she had a mate  
And was awaiting his arrival  
I laughed with her and wished her luck  
Hidden in me was the thought .. why? If only ..

Heard her voice in the aisle next to me  
was attracted to her and she reciprocated  
Talked and flirted with me while I kept thinking  
Wanting to find the right time and open up  
But didn’t know where to start and how  
Thought about all the thousand possible ways ..  
But before I did, she did .. quit  
the job and moved away  
Leaving me once again with .. if only
