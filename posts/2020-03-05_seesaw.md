The day scampers away  
 the night refuses to go away  
 you have walked away long ago  
 yet your memories refuse to move away

The thunder storm drenches me completely  
 every drop bringing stories of you tucked in it  
 as I struggle to hold them in my palm  
 It collapses and escapes from my clutches

You upset with me  
 me, angry at my heart  
 in this delicate state of mind  
 neither of us can comfort the other  
 nebulous is the world that we primed

my sagacious mind wants to let go  
 the irresponsible me holds to you tight  
 this tug of war has no real ending  
 leaving my life seesawing in muddled light
