I stood mesmerized  
 as she came running towards me  
 Her eyes transfixed only on me  
 while her name Maggie echoed  
 in the background

Standing next to me  
 she looked me up  
 Then went around me  
 stepping away and came back again  
 then decided I was not her type  
 and walked away

He chased after her  
 seeking her attention  
 calling her by name  
 and sometimes pleading for her  
 while she strolled in the pool of water  
 making him wait for her

Finally he caught up to her  
 irritated he was  
 grabbed her with his fist  
 and pulled her closer  
 subdued now, she responded  
 by wagging her tail  
 And dancing on her feet  
 forcing his anger to melt away
