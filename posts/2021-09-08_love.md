my naive brain believes in a love story .. countless movies and books have added to this euphoria .. a beautiful someone just walks in your life and turns it upside down .. surprisingly all my stories end in a tragedy ..  
  
  
> Alone and lonely was I   
> Seated on a bench, staring at the sky   
> In you walk, elegant and charming   
> Sit beside me and smile my way   
> I extend my arms to hold your hands   
> With an innocent smile and a toss of hair   
> You cuddle closer while I smell your air   
> With one arm around you,   
> The other lifts the face   
> A brief pause and you close your eyes   
> Our lips meet and in flows the energy   
> As our heart beats in sync with the kiss   
> I realize, I’m falling in love with you   
> The clock strikes a note, you push me away   
> Walk out my life, you choose the way   
>   
> - an excerpt from the poem ["Falling in Love", Nov 1997](1997_11_17_falling_in_love.html)*

  
.. and yet for all the love story madness, a partner is nothing but another person with a personality who each of us can define as ideal .. what I seek is a person who is simple ..   
  
  
> Hidden in the crowd is the simple girl  
> who lives the life for the joy it beholds  
> Smiling and oozing confidence, she dresses bright and bold  
> her warm personality beacons me to caress and hold  
> Yes ..yes says my heart she is the one .. she is the one   
>   
> - an excerpt from the poem ["Is she the one?", Aug 2009](2009_08_14_is_she_the_one.html)*

  
  
.. and at times, love takes a completely new dimension .. something beautiful and crazy .. a craziness that forces one to do things beyond imagination like the emperor who decided to build a monument that stands forever .. a labor of love .. one which has been chiseled on a white marble on a banks of a river .. the beautiful Taj Mahal ..   
  
  
> An ode to love   
> a white marble mausoleum   
> a tear drop for a beloved   
> built for eternity bloom   
>   
> - an excerpt from the poem ["Tajmahal", Jun 2021](2021_06_19_tajmahal.html)*
