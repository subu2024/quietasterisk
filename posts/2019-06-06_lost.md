She rolled on the bed ignoring the blaring alarm   
Grumpily pulling a pillow on her face and tossing around   
The many reason to continue to sleep allowed her to disarm   
Lost, she struggled to comprehend her life profound   
  
Soon the sun was blazing hot   
Everywhere the day was taking off shining a bright spot   
People scampered to run their lives like ordered by a despot   
While she sat with a phone, clicking through Facebook and Instagram bot   
  
Then one odd person called   
Talking about others as gossip piled across   
Later heading to the restaurant for a long lazy lunch   
While dawdling about others life like a boss   
  
As evening descended back in play   
Her family assembled back for the day   
She sat in her pajamas with disheveled hair grumpy not gay   
Her phone beeping with mundane text viewing life that never would stay   
  
Early in the evening, she retired for the night   
Complaining of a million things that never went right   
Pausing to yell at the slightest provoke and fight   
She hastily pulled the sheets with Netflix in tow and tight   
  
The purpose to live was completely lost   
The feeling of pity for self, engulfed her frost   
Hoping for a miracle that came with no cost   
She was lost unable to find her inner ghost
