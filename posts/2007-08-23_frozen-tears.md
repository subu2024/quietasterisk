title: "Frozen Tears"
date: 2007-08-23

excerpt: "A haunting poem about a child whose innocence is destroyed by a brutal act of violence."
---

The tears dried at the source  
 The time froze for the moment  
 The innocence was violently ripped away  
 The little boy will never be the same again

A random act of the perpetrators  
 Served no purpose for anyone  
 Dousing the child with gas and fire  
 Blatantly took the child from the little boy

His life would never be the same  
 The face scarred, heart broken  
 His friends shied away, school deserted him  
 At the age of five, the innocence was snatched away

He was still a child, knowing the world  
 In one brutal instant, they killed his desire  
 Filled his heart with hatred and spite  
 Took his trust of humans totally away

Alas what a shame!  
 In the name of religion and rights,  
 They robbed his fundamental rights  
 Leaving his tears dry at the source
