Behind the veil of cobwebs  
 her picture adorned the wall  
 Her smile radiant and unfazed  
 still set glow the dimly lit hall

Her memory lingered like a fragrance  
 long after she herself had left  
 The fifty blissful years that they shared  
 was something, only time and he could tell

He could see her, feel her  
 nothing in here did not stop reminding her  
 Her imprints everywhere,  
 resonated like thunder and made him shudder

The old lonely man searched the room  
 hoping to find her somewhere in there  
 oh! how he hoped for her one little touch  
 responding with a smile, rushing to his care

With a feeble voice, he prayed for her  
 sad she broke her promise to stay forever  
 Yet he knew, the choice was not hers  
 She would have stayed, if only she could maneuver

Remembering the days together, silently he cried  
 Tears poured down his sullen eyes  
 flowing calmly, hugging her footprints  
 erasing that one last piece of her!!\*
