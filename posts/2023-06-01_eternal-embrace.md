Fall in love with life, don't be shy,  
 Even if one day she'll say goodbye.  
 Secretly embrace the dark, sultry death,  
 She'll be with you every moment, every breath

Life and death, they go hand in hand,  
 So let's enjoy life, let's make it grand!  
 Let's dance and sing, let's have some fun,  
 For in the end, we'll all be one

Let's not fear death's embrace,  
 For it's a part of life's race  
 Let's live each day to the fullest,  
 And in death, find peace and rest
