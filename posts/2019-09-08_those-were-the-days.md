Those were the days  
 When we were in love and alive  
 You would wait for me  
 To run into my arms  
 You would look into my eyes  
 To dream and turn your charms  
 You would profess to stay  
 To eternity, forever with me always  
 Those were the days

Those were the days  
 When we were young and naive  
 You would kiss me tender  
 To stay locked with me  
 You would fight with me  
 To make up and surrender  
 You would hug me tight  
 To forget the world and blunder  
 Those were the days

Those were the days  
 When we were opposites and caroused all night  
 You were the sunshine  
 To the midnight sky that I was  
 You were the anchor  
 To the turbulence that I was  
 You were the destiny  
 To the pilgrim that I was  
 Those were the days
