 She was the silent one   
 Bringing me into this world of light   
 Always standing behind patiently   
 accepting as I grew from a child to an adult right   
   
 She never complained   
 Always soothing my pain with her calming anodyne   
 Standing as the epitome of patience   
 She was the mom that I was blessed pristine   
   
 And another was the aunt, inspiring strength   
 Encouraging me to take the leap   
 To define my own boundaries   
 Showing me that I can leap, fail and stand in the heap   
   
 She pushed me into the unknown   
 Forcing me to see the colors with a new beam   
 Taking me into areas between bright and gray   
 Allowing me to scale heights that I never would dream   
   
 They were the pillars in my life   
 Made me ready to face glory and pain   
 crashing suddenly one day within days apart   
 Leaving me gasping to learn to live again
