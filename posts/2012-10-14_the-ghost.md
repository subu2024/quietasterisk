I stand at the beach watching the sunset  
 The orange glow immersing slowly into the waves  
 Inherently I feel her standing next to me  
 Silently encompassing the evening with her frame

Burdened by mundane life, I gasp, my breath struggling  
 My eyes well up just crying no more  
 with a loving flutter of eternal love  
 She touches the palm of my hand yet remaining unseen

As I ponder on the absolute insanity of my life  
 Closeted in darkness in a silent world  
 Her footsteps interrupt my thoughts  
 Tip toeing to come near and wait in the dark

Wrapped in chambers of my heart are secrets  
 Squished in between are dreams that belong only to me  
 She reaches to tease and steal it from me  
 Planting a silent kiss cementing the tattered sleeve

The day I pine for togetherness  
 Lonely I sit with my crushed desires  
 She heals my heart and provides comfort  
 Letting rest my head on her invisible lap
