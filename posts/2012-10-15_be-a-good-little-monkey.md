  He tramped with a plan and glow  
  pouring water on the floor to watch the flow  
  Next he decided to wipe it clean  
  dragging the roll of toilet paper handy with white sheen  
  impressed with his job, he signaled his mom  
  aghast she yelled 'now be a good little monkey'  
   
  He climbed the kitchen pantry with an aim  
  searching for the candy all the way up the chain  
  He kicked the tray and bottle which stood his way  
  licking the dripping honey while smiling genuine and not fake  
  all this time she stood watching mesmerized  
  Now she yelled 'now be a good little monkey'  
   
  Sometimes silently he sat in front of the ipad  
  flipping pages here and there with his touch  
  pausing to watch his show before he wandered   
  immersed in this world, nothing bothered him, not even the doorbell  
  While she searched frantically everywhere  
  he responded to her call without moving anywhere  
  on his sight she remarked 'now be a good little monkey'  
   
  Night time brought a new flurry of activity  
  He climbed the staircase without stepping on the steps  
  and he closed the door with a bang  
  While pretending to brush his teeth  
  he excitedly jumped up and down on the bed   
  creating a ruckus that exhausted all others  
  getting him to sleep remained a big chore  
  angry yet amused, she remarked 'good night now be a good little monkey'
