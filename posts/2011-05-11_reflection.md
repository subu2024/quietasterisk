The cool droplets of water raining  
 Persuades the harsh sun to calm down finitely  
 Sensuously wrapping its arms around me  
 Shielding me from the solar glare sublimely

The sudden blowing of the gentle breeze  
 Whispers sweet nothings in my ear erotically  
 The hush hush schmooze of this affair interrupted only  
 By the sounds of the nearby brook flowing casually

The twilight change of the colors in the sky  
 Reveals the patterns of tones and hues delicately  
 Portraying her sensual love on this canvas vast and true  
 Coaxes me to pause and immerse in her dedication totally

The numerous trees all around bending their heads in deference  
 Nudges me into her delicate arms seemingly  
 The innumerable bridesmaid chirping in the background  
 Makes me fully rejoice in this royal coronation majestically

_Inspired by the British Royal Wedding_
