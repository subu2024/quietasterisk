Nature sings, time provides the ride  
 It is your unique journey in this tide  
 To leave behind your signature, your frequency, your pride  
 .. signature of your very own frequency and slide

The mortal cravings of everyday life  
 romancing with the music that you deeply adore  
 dancing to the rhythm of solitude and company  
 create that unique signature, playing silently in the fore

The journey till now, marks one milestone  
 with many more miles to go  
 To tread with abandoned joy and new hands to hold  
 some dear, others fear but the signature is yours, dear

Whether a wish, a dream or a nasty fall  
 the envelope of mystery wraps every life  
 for the universe has many ways to surprise  
 Driving you to the goal, following the frequency and no more

The latent energy will one day explode  
 The roads to achievement and fame will beacon to follow  
 Suppressing the morbid fear of age and death  
 To imprint your signature for time to swallow

In the enigmatic vastness of the universe  
 it is your tryst with destiny  
 creating emotions and experiences in the abyss of time and space  
 To echo in the cosmic black hole for years many
