I wake up in the morning and go tap tap tap tap  
 updating my status on Twitter, Facebook and Tumblr  
 Announcing to the world my quibbles, my fuss, my dripping sap

I sip my coffee and brush my teeth  
 While quickly sneaking in between an update indeed

I drive to work, my eyes on the road absconding  
 While enthusiastically reading and responding  
 to the unknown aunt on the picture of her cat chomping

I rush into work past the eyes staring  
 To write about the day and other stories not so daring

My lunch, my break, my walk, my dame  
 Every little nugget is direct to the web anyway  
 I have no shame or claim to fame  
 As I post everything not interesting and pretty mundane

I continue to post  
 About the dog, about Haiti, or the man sitting on the faraway titi  
 I really don’t care if you pass or read my graffiti

Every little happening, every nod every dash  
 I like to see it there bold, bright and ready to flash

Late in the night I pull myself onto my bed  
 My feeble eyes and legs pulling me astray  
 I desperately reach to get my handset from the tray  
 To go tap tap tap one more time on this day
