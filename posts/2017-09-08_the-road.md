  Up in the mountain hidden from sight  
  Seductive, serene yet desolate  
  The long winding road invites me  
  Down in the valley  
  The dirty crowded road   
  Bristling with people beckons me   
  While I stand at the crossroad searching for the road leading to you  
    
  While I stand at the crossroad   
  Searching for the road leading to you  
  asking where is the road leading to you  
    
  Sometimes I want to run on the hard gravel path  
  Only to collapse into your inviting open arm  
  Sometimes I want to crawl slowly on the sand covered path   
  Only to bury myself in your smiling charm  
  Sometimes mad and exhausted I sit watching, waiting  
  Until the demure happiness drags me to you from harm  
    
  As I traverse the many unchartered road narrow  
  I find the path of joy crisscrosses the trail of sorrow  
  My heart looks back to see the shards of memories in the barrow  
  While my eyes seek that simple path leading to you like farrow   
    
  Today I walk the path with not much thought  
  Delighted in the joy that the road has brought  
  Smelling the air reminding everything I ever sought   
  For I know the journey may lead on path with danger fraught  
  the destination may change; but life with you will never be naught
