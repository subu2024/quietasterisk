title: "Broken Leg"

excerpt: "A tragic reflection on abandonment and loneliness when an injured woman finds herself forgotten by family."
---

There she was  
 walking with him, hand in hand  
 until she fell with a thud and pause  
 Her legs never again let her stand

Suddenly she saw  
 Alone was she, totally helpless  
 The world moved on without a tear  
 Unnoticed was a face, just merciless

Her own kith and kin,  
 Her blood and sweat  
 Her own creation, her children  
 Missing her, were they or not, who bet

Immersed in their own life  
 Left her alone, writhing in pain  
 Was it the leg or her heart  
 Only she could tell but in vain

All along her world with no blur  
 Seemed black and white and no color  
 Alone she pondered and cursed  
 Her leg, her heart, her soul

In this dark time,  
 She cried, she cried, she cried  
 Her voice reaching only her eyes  
 Her legs weak, not anymore willing to try
