Time my love, has played a cruel joke  
 Bringing us together on the dreams of hope  
 Leaving the souls today, standing alone and broke  
 With our own wretched demons to cope

The season of youth masked the many flaws  
 Pausing for seconds on tender moments and applause  
 Yet today we stand, many questions unanswered, in claws  
 Unsure, what got resolved, what torments, what was?

The strange minutes of happy togetherness  
 Quickly dissolved in the muddiness of the forever madness  
 Standing in the shadows of the ever after blackness  
 Far away the future beholds another day of unhappiness

With no clarity what am I searching for in this twilight  
 With no idea which path to travel in spite  
 I walk silently, absolutely no destination in sight  
 Weaving dreams of hope every moment in darkness and light
