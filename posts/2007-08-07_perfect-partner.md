She yearned for him  
 Her eyes scanning the horizon  
 to quickly zoom into him  
 for in him she found her perfect partner

She could not wait to chat  
 to drench him in her emotional rain  
 Narrate every moment of her day  
 to drown him in her sorrow, joy and stain

She longed to be consoled by him  
 Basking in his manly shade, she loved to feel secure and safe  
 She rejoiced in sharing her privacy  
 She felt complete just being with him

Somewhere in the secure world  
 She met, flirted and fell for him  
 ignoring logic and better sense  
 She lost her self completely to him

Sometimes she dreamt of the hot steamy nights  
 Her face buried in his naked chest  
 His arms wrapped around her back and breast  
 While his lips kissing her cascade of hair

Emotionally she entwined in him, closing the divide  
 Forgetting his virtual existence, his online self  
 She completely leaned on him as her true spouse  
 while her ignorant husband snored in the bed nearby
