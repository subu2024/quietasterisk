Her long black hair, a silken tide,  
 Calls out to touch, to pull inside  
 It frames her face, a radiant glow,  
 A treasure I long to hold and know

Her eyes, deep brown, full of cheer,  
 Sparkling with dreams that draw me near -  
 though warm with cheer, have shadows deep,  
 In quiet moments, they reveal the secrets without shear

Her lips curve, a smile that gently blooms,  
 Like petals kissed by morning’s rooms  
 Whispering softly, sweet and true,  
 Each word she speaks, time starts anew

Her cheeks blush warm, a soft glow there,  
 A radiant charm beyond compare  
 Like moonlight, she guides through darkest skies,  
 Turning night to day before my eyes

Her voice, a river’s calm, a quiet tide,  
 Yet beneath, a storm that swells with the tide  
 At times, a glance from her can break my peace,  
 But in her gaze, my breath finds sweet release

Her steps graceful, light, and free,  
 A dance that calms and comforts me  
 Her presence eclipses all I know  
 A symphony that makes my heart glow

Her face, like a thought, a fleeting picture,  
 A dream’s own riddle, wrapped in pure conjecture  
 A moment of certainty, yet shrouded in doubt,  
 A cheerful enigma, flawless throughout

Her laughter echoes through my soul,  
 Like notes of a song that time can't control  
 She stands, a vision, both fierce and sweet,  
 My heart, my love, where all worlds meet
