 Old, baffled, totally in disarray   
 power of thinking shrunk and gone astray   
 The once mighty, now a poor self and fray   
 hanging to dear life, living shrunken and gray   
   
 Looking back, the days when she ruled by bray   
 Pushing people to her beck and cray   
 living life on her terms in her day   
 nonchalantly pushing others from her tray   
   
 Many lost battles colored her life   
 Yet she rose above this to strive   
 Her confidence peppered with skills and pride   
 Kept her moving against all odds and will and tide   
   
 She made friends, she made enemies   
 People fought to get in her company and limelight   
 Such was the aura that pulled many into spotlight   
 Yet she callously pushed them when she decided to change the light   
   
 Now in the sunset of her life   
 She struggled to live her daily life   
 Alone and isolated she sat and cried   
 Wishing for wishes that may never materialize
