Second amendment .. a constitutional right to bear arms .. fully support this right .. yet the intention was right when made but now, it is past its time .. a change is needed .. We live in a society with rules, laws and a judiciary system not the jungles of 1700’s .. guns are a must for law enforcement people not ordinary citizens let alone immature and troubled youths .. yet our elected officials refuse to change .. the ignorance persists while lives .. innocent lives are at stake .. kids being killed in large numbers due to this “right to bear arms” .. ignorant and troubled people getting hold of weapons that don't belong in society .. when will we see the winds of change?

  
  
  
 The little girl   
 turned around   
 startled   
 Time stood still   
 the bullet raced towards her   
 inching its way   
 Her eyes opened wide   
 voice loud   
 Yet no sound made   
 She stretched her arms   
 to grab   
 whatever   
 next to her   
 but fell   
   
 Closing her eyes   
 she screamed   
 “Mommy”   
   
 The bullet pierced   
 her chest   
 Ripping the skin,   
 the flesh   
 splashing blood,   
 breaking   
 her bones   
   
 She lay   
 on the ground   
 innocent   
 eyes open   
 still dreaming   
   
 Time stood still   
 a moment becoming an eternity ..  
   
   
 - "Bullet", May 2022
