excerpt: "A somber poem about relationships slowly eroding as time and changing priorities build emotional distance."
---

Time creates the divide  
 You and I will recognize  
 Growing slowly apart  
 We let a chasm build an invisible wall

Priorities change, likes give way to annoyance  
 What once seemed the lovable quirks  
 Now takes all the scarce resources  
 Draining the last sap of the still remaining energy

Together we planned to lead a life  
 Now it is forced circumstantially  
 This loveless life drags on aimlessly  
 Leaving but bad taste all around

Sometimes I wish this will not last  
 My life will break free to free all  
 But the rhythm of the relationship  
 Dances to the daily quibbles of life and death with no recall
