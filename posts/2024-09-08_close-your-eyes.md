 Rushing through the bustle, the days feel the same,   
 Could you pause today, just a second, step off the fast lane?   
 Close your eyes, breathe deep, and forget the game,   
 Think of me now, just a flicker, a name   
   
 In the haze of the past, feel the love that remained,   
 See the man you glanced over, moments unexplained   
 The treasures you once had, now ghostly and waned,   
 All the things that slipped away, silently drained   
   
 You will then smile, tinted with rue,   
 Knowing full well what your choices led to—   
 The heart that you hurt, but never quite knew   
 And then you can say,   
 Life keeps moving, no pause to complain  
   
 But just for a moment—remember me, my pain   
 Close your eyes, breathe deep ..
