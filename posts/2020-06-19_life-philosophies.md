I let go the need to be perfect   
Illusion it is, to aim for this object   
Mistakes happen every day but call it not a defect   
Win or lose, it is my life beautifully imperfect   
   
I let go of self-criticism   
talking to myself, treating self with disdain   
The life I create instead   
Waking every day to fall and rise again   
   
I let go blaming   
‘cause Im not a victim, nor helpless   
This is my life   
I’m what I believe, living my dreams and plus   
   
I let go judging   
Never want to react   
to your opinion and rage   
To give power away, over my life enact   
   
I let go making assumptions   
Telling stories, letting my mind play a game   
Simply taking a walk to breathe fresh air   
Makes me want to accept life with dignity and shame   
   
I let go chasing happiness   
It is not a destination I seek   
But a journey where I walk and smile   
Sometimes letting others have a quick peek   
   
I let go worrying about the far away future   
All I have is today   
Yesterday is gone, tomorrow may never rupture   
Live I do in full, every moment every day
