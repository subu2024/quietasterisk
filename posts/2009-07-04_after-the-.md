He saw her attractive figure  
 Instantly his heart raced to meet her  
 They latched like two lost lovers  
 Kissing to satisfy the inner devil

He grabbed her hair and tore her bra  
 She sucked him with a force unknown  
 They sank into each other  
 Mesmerized with zero pride

Biting tearing and fully immersed  
 They squeezed the last drop of the sweet fleeting pleasure  
 The goddess of lust entwined with the devils of death  
 Danced to the tune of the karmic passion

And then ..  
 what an instant fall was it  
 He felt dizzy, used, spent  
 Drained emotionally and physically

The sex was great, the result unknown  
 She dressed again to look attractive  
 He smiled, she hugged  
 She kissed, he tasted

She walked out; he stood looking at the shadow  
 Nothing had changed yet something was  
 Disgust penetrated his body like a foul smell  
 He wallowed in pity at his own lust
