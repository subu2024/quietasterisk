> This summer day, the long arching wings of the tree   
> shielding me from the blaze of the furious sun   
> keeping me cool in the afternoon time   
> providing the comfort that I dare not shun   
>   
> The green branches take the heat   
> ah! I said as I lay under the tree   
> feeling the ever warm winds brush through my face   
> Frantically covering my face with a wet towel with grace   
>   
> an old squirrel running from afar paused to study me   
> wanting to get under the shade onto the peat   
> looking undecided pensively peering at me   
> finally leaping across to the branch to escape the heat   
>   
> drinking a sip of cold water   
> I dare not set foot outside my seat   
> suddenly hearing that loud thunder   
> I smiled waiting for the downpour to calm the heat
