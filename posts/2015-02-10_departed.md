He lay on the bed with his face frail  
 revealing the shine of that unknown peace  
 He was long gone on that one way trail  
 Departing .. leaving behind many unfinished business amiss

She struggled to gather her surroundings  
 Thousand emotions raced to garner her attention  
 The tug of the mundane life was real and binding  
 While she wanted time to grieve without any apprehension

The others sauntered in many ways  
 Some cared, wanting to help, some there, just to be there  
 Few others hoped to share the faded glory days  
 Carrying back shards of gossip to spread every other stare

The children stood silently in the corner  
 Not comprehending the tragedy that departed the authority  
 The many noises masked their sublime emotions  
 harshly flinging them across the threshold into the age of majority

Slowly time crawled forward  
 All the many that came left their way  
 Leaving behind the new empty vacuum  
 for her to embrace and cry on the remembrance day
