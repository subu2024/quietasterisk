---

  
  
  With desire in her eyes,   
  she feasted on him  
  In the crowded running train  
  Her eyes were just for him  
  Ignorantly he stood, far from her  
  Deep in thought just like in trance  
  She did everything to get his attention  
  Her quick glance, her nervous smile  
  The little movement of legs, crossing over her thighs  
  She made the slightest noise just for him  
    
  In the afternoon rush time, the train came to a halt  
  People skirted in and out   
  Her time stood still as she paused  
  Walking past him, she brushed open her hair  
  Covering his face for a brief second,  
  Her smell lingered while she got down the train  
  Stepping away from him, she smiled  
  With a quick glance over her shoulder  
  She saw him still in trance  
  As the train left the platform,   
  She laughed with desire in her eyes
