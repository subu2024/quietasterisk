Our place on earth, our universe .. magnificent, beautiful and the only place we can call home. Yet the universe’s size is baffling.. so big that we cannot comprehend nor wrap our arms around .. the numbers are mind-boggling big that we cannot even try to perceive .. just lumping it under light-years and astronomical units .. Even light traveling at its best will take years to reach the nearest galaxy .. again, the extremes are reached on the smaller size too with atoms, electrons, protons .. tiny, very tiny that we cannot understand nor see .. atoms made of nothing but empty space yet powerful to create everything that we know, see, touch and feel .. In between these extreme wonders, we live with our thoughts, ideas, feelings, emotions and self-inflicted problems .. It's all [Maya](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/maya_(religion)) .. an illusion or is it?
