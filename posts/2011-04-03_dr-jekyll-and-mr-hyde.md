Looking into the mirror  
 As I brushed my teeth  
 I saw my twin  
 Stare back with an evil grin  
 Ha! I said, I want to do good  
 Hmm! He laughed, you really do!  
 I'm always at your side looking out for you  
 While you goof and want to do someone good  
 Naive was I "can you help me now?"  
 No! He said let us screw them all  
 The fun is better to see them in pain  
 While you and I sit back and soak in the rain  
 Please no! I really want to make a difference  
 Nah said the masked twin. Never under my watch  
 I begged and pleaded with mixed result  
 He laughed while I cringed in solitary shame

All decisions that I make  
 All choices that I pick  
 is a battle between these twins  
 my inner Dr Jekyll and Mr Hyde  
 Two sides of a coin, two lives of me  
 The good and the bad bound together  
 Finding a ground to survive is beautifully insane
