 The many vivid dreams   
 Fade like flowers, flowers dropping from their stems,   
 Happiness quietly dissolves,   
 painfully pricking my heart   
 Like thorns piercing deep   
 With the tick of seconds, splendors are lost   
 Like the departure of spring,   
 Watching in a haze the transition, while   
 time slips away with an unflinching smile   
   
 Leaves fall in silence,   
 Branches burn in red,   
 Desire remains unfulfilled,   
 I cry, cry over things that never materialized, while   
 time slips away with an unflinching smile   
   
 Songs turn into tears   
 Dreams are buried hidden   
 Companions vanish like smoke   
 Despite these losses,   
 Life halts, halts at every turn,   
 To watch the ups and downs while   
 time slips away with an unflinching smile   
   
 I marvel at the beauty of youth,   
 When love blossoms, blossoms like flowers,   
 And my reflection in the mirror dazzles   
 Earth and sky merge in unity,   
 My heart leaps at every sight   
 And then, the wind blows,   
 Stunned, I helplessly watch   
 The evening disappearing into the night, while   
 time slips away with an unflinching smile   
   
 My hand reaches out to the dark tresses,   
 Lips search for the human touch,   
 Pain deeply hidden in love,   
 Breathing in slowly to seek eternal peace   
 Slowly leaving, leaving behind   
 A life that never truly was, while   
 time slips away with an unflinching smile
