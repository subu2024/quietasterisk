 When   
 The world seems scarier   
 news surrounding you grimmer   
 past trauma bubbling up faster   
 shame and guilt cuddle and smother   
   
 Even   
 while your soul is bruised and battered   
 A lifetime chasing dreams faltered   
 viewing a slow motion life of tragedy battered   
 finding yourself in existential exhaustion shattered   
 darkness enveloping your only life   
   
 Be   
 optimistic   
 swim in the waters of hope   
 transcend the noise in your head   
 look within yourself   
 move attention to the present   
 accept your imperfect life   
 become one with the universe   
   
 Await   
 the incoming light after darkness
