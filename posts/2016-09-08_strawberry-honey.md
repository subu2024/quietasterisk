  Shining brightly in crystal white  
  she wrapped her arms around me  
  looking directly into my eyes  
  she shook her head albeit with a smile  
  I have never danced this way before  
  seeing you super-high, I feel wimpy-low  
  sweeping her amber curls revealing her face in heaven  
  she said this has never happened since sixty seven  
  with the time-difference in between I cannot be seen with you  
  teasing me she pulled herself away  
  as I tugged her close  
  don't leave me now my strawberry honey  
  stay with me this summer and many  
  my days will grow shorter without you honey  
  I may not see you like this again for years far many  
  moving closer and bridging the gap grainy  
  she kissed my cheek once many  
  while quickly planting a smooch on my red hot lip shiny  
  I have to go  
  I have to go  
  saying she hugged me tight  
  we were never meant to tango  
  I'm just a dream  
  a faraway dream  
  she pushed me away  
  watching me fall  
  all the way into the night's dark fray

*Inspired by the phenomenon of full moon on the begin of summer solstice - first time since 1967*
