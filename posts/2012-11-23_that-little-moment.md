That little moment  
 Stood still  
 As memories flooded in  
 Tears rushed out  
 That moment frozen  
 Stood still

As night passed by  
 That lonely feeling  
 Clutched me tight, never letting go  
 As clock ticked forward  
 That little moment  
 Refused to die

If you were with me  
 Will that change?  
 Will the warmth from you make it dissipate?  
 I don't know, do I?  
 That little moment  
 Refused to go

That little moment  
 When everything turned black  
 Light firmly eclipsed  
 I searched for you, just you  
 That little moment  
 Refused to let me live

That little moment  
 Stood still
