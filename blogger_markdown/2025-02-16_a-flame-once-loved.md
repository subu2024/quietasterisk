 Sitting by the window, gazing at the rain,   
 I thought of the flame that once eased my pain   
 Its warmth was fleeting, but still, it stayed,   
 Lingering in shadows where memories played   
   
 Walking through the garden, flowers in bloom,   
 I felt the weight of an unseen gloom   
 The petals whispered of a time now gone,   
 A love that burned but couldn’t hold on   
   
 Lying in the dark, the quiet stretched deep,   
 The scars of longing refused to sleep   
 Each wound, a story, etched in my chest,   
 A tale of love that wouldn’t find rest   
   
 Waking at dawn as the sun gently rose,   
 The ember of yearning silently glows   
 Though the flame has faded, its call still near,   
 A love once lost, forever remains dear
