 Startled I awoke in my dreams  
 She was sitting next to me   
 A smile adorned her face   
 Simple, elegant, seraphic, magnetic   
 She was the epitome of beauty   
   
 Confused, muddled, perplexed   
 Discordant thoughts rising, I gaped   
 She responded with a comforting nod   
 My affinity for you will not change ever   
   
 Placing her delicate hands on my forehead   
 Soothing, pacifying, comforting   
 She questioned my turmoil state   
 I’m with you even if you don’t agree   
   
 Emboldened I asked her identity   
 I’m your mother that nourishes you   
 I’m your sibling that cares for you   
 I’m your lover that makes love to you   
 I’m your shadow that stays with you   
 I’m anger, I’m hate, I’m love, I’m joy   
 I’m the creator, I’m the destroyer   
 I’m the one with a thousand names   
   
 I’m present whether you accept or disown me   
 I’m in front and behind of you   
 I’m seen and unseen by you   
 I’m easy to please and hard to run away from   
 Omnipotent, powerful, divine   
 I’m always with you here there everywhere   
   
 And she continued   
 While I stared with bafflement   
 Her words resonating like a known felicitous lullaby   
 tranquilizing and sending joyful ecstasy brine   
 From the top of my head down my spine   
   
 Suddenly waking up, I saw her   
 Radiant, poised and charming   
 Leaning forward, she embraced me   
 Allowing her scent to perforate through every part of me   
 My hands feeling the velvet touch of her black hair   
 Drawing me closer, She whispered I’m life   
 Embrace me without conditions just like I do   
 Then she was gone while I remained   
 Forlorn, blissful and musing   
 My hands clinging to an empty black space
