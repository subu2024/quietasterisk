---
title: I Traded You for a Ghost
date: 2025-12-06
category: Love, Loss, and Time
featured: true
archived: true
---

I traded you for a ghost

You were here,  
Next to me  
I could share  
A joke  
A smile  
A story  
My body-  
And everything me

But I let you go  
I traded you for a ghost,  
Quietly crowned in frost.

Now the nights are cold,  
shadows surround me  
I reach in the dark,  
for a hand,  
a voice,  
a spark of breath—  
but only silence receives me

I realize  
I traded you for a ghost  
And the ghost truly loves me

And then  
the ghost moves in  
lying silently in my bed  
teaching me to forget warmth  
I share again  
A joke  
A smile  
A story  
And nothing mine

I remember  
I traded you for a ghost,  
And now the shadow  
secretly  
wears my face
