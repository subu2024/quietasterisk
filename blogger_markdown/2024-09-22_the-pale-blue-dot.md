 Floating in the dark endless sea of space   
 A tiny blue speck, my home, my place   
   
 This pale blue dot, my world, my own   
 Here I am, forever known   
   
 Many have walked, many will go   
 I'm just a brief part of this earthly show   
   
 Where are you rushing, oh wanderer dear?   
 All you need is here, don't fear   
   
 This is the heaven, the hell, the everything true   
 In her embrace, we will find our due   
   
 To love her, to cherish her, should be our quest   
 In her cradle, we will finally rest   
   
 What tomorrow brings, I cannot say   
 But here, in her arms, comfortable, I stay   
   
 In the end, in her tender embrace   
 I'll find my peace, my final eternal place
