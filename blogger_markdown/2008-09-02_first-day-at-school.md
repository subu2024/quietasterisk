  Your first day at school  
  Excited as you were  
  Totally oblivious were you  
  of the new world, new pool  
    
  You were ready at the get go  
  in all your gears and brimming to go  
  Somehow I expected you to be scared  
  So I can be the dad and show you how to row  
    
  At the right time, you jumped in  
  With brimming innocence and unbound eagerness  
  As much as I was scared to see you there  
  You showed bravery far ahead your age  
    
  Just a brief hesitation, as you made new friends  
  Starting a new journey, your own world  
  Left was I standing and waving a bye  
  Not believing that you are ready to grow
