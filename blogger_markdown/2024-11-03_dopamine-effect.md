 Every year, we wait,   
 a buzz in the air,   
 the promise of something new,   
 the iPhone’s glow on the horizon   
 We check the website,   
 hearts racing,   
 ready to click and buy,   
 a small joy igniting—   
 then, a drop,   
 as autumn settles in,   
 and the thrill fades   
   
 In love, it’s the same,   
 the sweet game of waiting,   
 the chase through calls and texts,   
 building up to that first touch, the first kiss,   
 But soon, reality comes,   
 the person beneath the shine,   
 and life’s troubles creep in,   
 reminding us we’re human   
   
 We keep moving,   
 chasing small thrills,   
 each moment a spark,   
 until we reach a peak,   
 only to find ourselves   
 back where we started—   
 the dance begins anew,   
 the cycle of hope and return,   
 pushing us to seek, the next   
 fleeting dopamine effect
