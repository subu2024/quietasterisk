> Standing at the doors of the jungle  
>  peering into the unknown dark horizon  
>  beholding the strange world work and fumble  
>  you smile with joy and leap into the amazon  
>    
>  unaware of the adventure far ahead  
>  waiting to sweep you with absolute no attention   
>  whisking you into glory and beyond  
>  tossing few measures of tears and joy in unison apprehension  
>    
>  sometimes you will fail   
>  the fall louder than you can bear  
>  getting up again and grabbing the rein  
>  is what you unknowingly prepared all this growing days in rear  
>    
>  sometimes you will sail  
>  far ahead than anyone may care  
>  leaving behind many precious gems in the dark pail  
>  such is the fun of this unknown dare  
>    
>  as for today, not just another day  
>  cause you step from the comfort of the childhood care  
>  into the dark abyss of future fame and stare  
>  to find your path as you win, fall, fail and stay in the glare
