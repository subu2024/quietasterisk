> Sitting in the crowded train  
>  Letting wild thoughts run in the brain  
>  Watching the I66 get blanketed with rain  
>  Counting the stopped traffic and drivers in vain  
>  As the train veers into the tunnel, peering with strain  
>  I see only the reflection of two little boys playing train  
>  A new stop brings more into the terrain  
>  Jostling for space, pushing each other with restrain  
>  Smiling to hide the anxious anger from drain  
>  The smell in the hot space runs fowl giving me migraine   
>  Oh! The driver announces delay with a casual grain  
>  More jostling and murmurs but all eyes still glued to the iPad strain  
>  I can do nothing trapped but smile sitting in the crowded train
