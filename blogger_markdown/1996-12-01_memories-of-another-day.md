---
title: Memories of Another Day
date: '1996-12-01'
category: Life & Uncertainty
featured: true
archived: true
---

Running with the mad crowd in this restless world  
I pause and look back, one fine day  
Memories cloud me while I gasp for breath  
Faces whiz past, so many, old and new  
Few that I remember, others that I tried to forget  
They laugh and mock at me  
As I cry into the shoulders of time  
Childhood, adolescence, people, places  
Family and friends, each has a story to tell  
With the light of memory, I search  
The jungles of the past, looking for footprints  
Faces, known and unknown, shine now and then  
Cuddled in the arms of time, cozy and warm  
While I watch alone shivering in the present  
Yet the rush is on and I have to go  
The future beckons me while the past will not let go  
I hug the fading past and run along  
Tears tucked away and smile to show  
Meeting more faces along the way  
Miles and miles to go before I can pause and slow  
To think about THE good old days!
