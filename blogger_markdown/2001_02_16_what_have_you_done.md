---
title: What have you done for me?
date: '2001-02-16'
category: Love, Loss, and Time
featured: true
archived: true
---

Deep in the heart  
 Etched for life  
 Echo it did  
 In every nerve and vein  
 A remark so callously uttered  
 ‘What have you done for me?’  
 So she said point blank  
 ‘I’m not happy in this house’  
 All the blame is on you  
 And she meant with a cold voice  
 So she broke  
 This fragile heart  
 Unexpected she made it happen  
 This statement that meant to say  
 She cared less,  
 All these tears  
 That clung to her feet  
 But could not stop  
 So she ruined this  
 It was her life  
 So she blamed  
 The only one  
 Who stood for her  
 The words were said  
 The deed was done  
 And now she smiled  
 But the remark resounded again and again  
 And something broke  
 Which she did not see!
