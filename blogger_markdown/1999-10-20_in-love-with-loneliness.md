---
title: In love with Loneliness
date: '1999-10-20'
category: Love, Loss, and Time
featured: true
archived: true
---

A damsel called Loneliness  
 captivates me with her dark smile

In the hush-hush quiet of the night,  
 Whispers sweet-nothings in my ear

Holds me close to her full breast  
 Until there is nothing between me and her

Entwines me with her lush long hair,  
 Eerie in nature and jet black everywhere

I see only her here, there, everywhere  
 We are company from me to her

Quietly she moves away, while I sleep  
 Her tiptoe foot croons a silent lullaby

I pine for her nearness, a heart broken lover  
 While she abandons me with cold charm

Seducing in secret and abandoning in gay  
 She adds more chapters to her dark history
