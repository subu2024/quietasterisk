---
title: One Way Street
date: '2009-09-06'
category: Life & Uncertainty
featured: true
archived: true
---

  
  I saw the road ahead clear  
  Holding my child, I marched with no fear  
  Behind me was the parent of me dear  
  Ignorant remained I refusing to look back in the window rear  
    
  Totally engrossed in my duties near  
  I enjoyed the moment, time and year  
  Feeling the bond of the love adhere  
  I frolicked in the sun, the rain and the lovely river  
    
  Time moved on, not stopping for any prayer  
  As the past raced to catch the present year  
  Slowly wisdom rained on me to make it crystal clear   
  It is just a one way street from past to present forever  
    
  No matter how much I cry or offer prayer  
  I will not hold the present move prior  
  My ignorance of past present, the next generation adheres   
  Leaving me to walk the lonely one way street with a tear
