Many ideas that bubble up into a thought but seldom gets completed .. like incomplete houses .. skeletons that need a lot of work .. yet at one point in time,  it seemed it had wings .. reviewing some of the incomplete poems in my repository .. some incomplete because I could not find the next logical step .. some due to sheer laziness .. few others, just lost in time and space .. So, here I took an almost completed version and asked ChatGPT to help and voila! we have a finished product .. a first for me in collaboration with [ChatGPT](https://openai.com/blog/chatgpt) .. Impressed with the way the last line shaped up ..

.. and even the image is AI generated .. welcome to the new world!
