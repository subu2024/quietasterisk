You’re beautiful,    
I don’t just mean the way you look—    
it’s in how you laugh, how you move,    
how you turn a room with just your hook  

The way your hair catches the light,    
it’s kinda wild, kinda right    
You’ve got that easy kind of grace    
that makes the world slow down,    
makes me forget my place  

You’re not just want—    
you’re my need    
You’re the rhythm in my heartbeat,    
the air when I breathe  

When you glance my way?    
it’s like the universe takes a breath—    
pauses for a second    
just to see what you’ll do next  

Your brown eyes?    
Deep—like stories told in candlelight    
I look, and I’m gone,    
lost in that endless night  

And your lips—they’re poetry in motion   
Soft, sweet.. like secrets I’ve always wanted  
You speak and I’m undone—    
like gravity forgot how to run  

Your hair’s a wave I want to drown in,    
a midnight sky I want to lie within  
And I swear, when you smile,    
it’s like something sacred begins  

You’re my fire,    
my calm,    
my golden hour glow    
You’re every song I hum    
and didn’t know I know  

You’re beautiful  
.. You’re beautiful
