Our intricate and enigmatic brain, a wondrous gift bestowed upon us, holds immense potential and yet presents a conundrum .. bestowing upon us the unparalleled ability to scrutinize information, strategize, envision, and pioneer. This extraordinary faculty allows us to indulge in the delightful luxury of envisioning a future .. a future brimming with goodness and greatness. However, the same brain prowess also grants us the ability to wander into the abyss of pessimistic reveries, where we conjure up dire scenarios that do not exist .. tormenting our very souls. At times, our imagination even dares to make the impossible frighteningly real.

In moments like these, we yearn for something that grounds us .. that steers our wandering mind back to reality, preventing it from galloping down the wrong path.

**God is that definition* that helps bring us back to the reality .. the present world. God is the embodiment of our salvation, reminding us to harness the power of our brain and rein it in when it threatens to veer off course.*

Do you recall that exquisite sensation just before you drift off into slumber? The sublime tranquility that washes over you, cocooning you in a state of bliss? Scientists label this state of mind the alpha state—an exquisite symphony of brain waves that meander at a slower pace. It is within this magical realm that we experience God .. that cherished feeling of bliss.

God embodies the concept that beckons us to acknowledge the irrefutable truth .. all we truly possess is this very moment—the present. The past and the future are mere abstractions conjured by our ceaselessly ruminating brain. Often, it behooves us to simply embrace the here and now, neither resisting nor fleeing from it, but rather approaching it with an unquenchable curiosity .. to exist fully, to be truly alive and not lost in the labyrinthine corridors of time.

In the perplexing world of morality, we often find ourselves adrift, grappling with the delineation between right and wrong .. our souls teetering on the precipice of moral dilemmas .. desperately seeking an answer. God becomes our personal compass .. an invisible line of morality we are unwilling to cross. God is the concept that remains deeply personal .. it is within our own hearts that we draw this line.

God serves as the intangible boundary that separates the land of science and the unknown .. the dichotomy between good and evil .. the demarcation between the familiar and the uncharted .. encompassing everything that eludes our comprehension .. everything our heart yearns for ..

God is a concept that defies definition .. encompassing the infinite possibilities of our boundless imaginations .. interestingly, [all residing within us](2019_08_19_she.html).
