.. sometimes, love can be stupid .. seeing a silhouette of a person, the mind wanders and conjures up a complete love story .. it does not even pause for a reason ..   
  

> *As I walked past the door*    
> *Her pretty frame was the only one visible*   
> *in the darkness of the early morning*    
> *The light behind making her radiant to glow*    
>   
> - an excerpt from the poem [the flirt, aug 2010](2010_08_16_the_flirt.html)

  
And this stupidity doesn’t stop with age .. it matures .. like this story of a old guy falling in love with a younger person and chasing her ..   
  
> *Our eyes met for the first time at a wedding harbor*     
> *Me an old man and she many many years younger*     
> *Lust penetrated the gap as she blushed each time I walked past her*     
> *Under the dark night, I grabbed her, planting a kiss drawing her closer*    
>   
> - an excerpt from the poem [the old man and the girl, aug 2018](2018_08_17_the_old_man_and_the_girl.html)

  
the obsession can slowly take the path of deeper feelings like depression and loneliness .. a need remains unfulfilled while it seems the world around is satiated in awesome love and happy endings ..  
  
> *A damsel called Loneliness*     
> *captivates me with her dark smile*     
>   
> - an excerpt from the poem [in love with loneliness, oct 1999](1999_10_20_in_love_with_loneliness.html)

  
  
And we enter the realms of nature .. falling in love with the moon, the rain ..   
  
> *Shining brightly in crystal white*    
> *she wrapped her arms around me*    
> *looking directly into my eyes*    
> *she shook her head albeit with a smile*    
> *I have never danced this way before*    
> *seeing you super-high, I feel wimpy-low*  

> - an excerpt from the poem, [Strawberry Honey](2016_09_08_strawberry_honey.html)

  
 even the British royal wedding did not fail to inspire another of this inane writing

> *The cool droplets of water raining*    
> *Persuades the harsh sun to calm down finitely*    
> *Sensuously wrapping its arms around me*    
> *Shielding me from the solar glare sublimely*  

> - an excerpt from the poem [Reflection, may 2011](2011_05_11_reflection.html)

  
Ah! the many forms of love .. yet nothing is real .. love is the feeling generated within you .. the other elements are merely a trigger ..
