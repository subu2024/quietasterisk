 An ode to love   
 a white marble mausoleum   
 a tear drop for a beloved   
 built for eternity bloom   
   
 On the banks of river Yamuna   
 A monument sophisticated in architecture   
 built by Shah Jahan   
 Taking more than 16 years   
 Standing today   
 as a poem for his beautiful beloved queen   
   
 Standing on the sands of time   
 The white marble tomb glistening far as eye can see   
 The pristine beautiful monument of love   
 Holding buckets of tears   
 and sadness wrapped deep inside   
 Dreams that died early   
 Memories that stayed lost in time   
   
 Today we watch in awe   
 This symmetrical marble tomb   
 Priding on its structure   
 taking pictures   
 Remembering stories   
 of a love that lived and got buried in pages of history
