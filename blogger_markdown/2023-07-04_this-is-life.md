 This is life, a gentle melody   
 Not a game of triumph or defeat   
 Not a race where you can retreat   
 It beckons us with its solemnity   
 This is life, a tapestry to weave   
   
 It whispers of acceptance, profound   
 Embracing each moment, big or small   
 Not merely seeking a curtain call   
 But finding solace in what is found   
 This is life, where hearts enthrall   
   
 It's not a quest for accolades high   
 Nor a battlefield to conquer and sway   
 It's the rhythm that guides us each day   
 With grace, we live and love, oh my   
 This is life, where passions sway   
   
 In its gentle cadence, we discover   
 The beauty of both joy and strife   
 An ever-evolving dance of life   
 This is life, where souls recover   
 With its tender touch, we find life
