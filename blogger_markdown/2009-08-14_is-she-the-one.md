title: Is she the one?
date: '2009-08-14'
category: Love, Loss, and Time
featured: true
archived: true
---

  That girl with the loud lipstick  
  Her hair set in the salon perfect with zero nick  
  Her dress oozing the fashion shtick  
  with a smile that portrayed a wicked uptick  
  Is she the one, the question I ask  
    
  Another girl, shabbily dressed  
  Her hair where every strand looked depressed  
  The colors obscure she wore matched plain cypress  
  with zero smile displayed oppressed  
  Is she the one, the question I ask  
    
  Here comes another who looks bright  
  Wait! Something about her feels not right!  
  She frowns and scowls wanting me to run out of sight  
  Her legs, body, smile with no rhythm day or night  
  Is she the one, the question I ask  
    
  Hidden in the crowd is the simple girl  
  Who lives the life for the joy it beholds  
  Smiling and oozing confidence, she dresses bright and bold  
  her warm personality beacons me to caress and hold  
  Yes .. yes says my heart she is the one .. she is the one
