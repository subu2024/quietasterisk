  Their eyes focused on the iPhone  
  hands typing vigorously  
  oblivious to their surroundings  
  They sat like zombies in the metro passively  
    
  With a white cable dangling from their ears  
  a weird noise blaring untimely  
  Their feet tapping to silent rhythm  
  they were lost in their gung-ho world frequently  
    
  In this wonderland, it was raining zombies in the early morning  
  they came in shapes and all size  
  Men and women and even toddlers at times  
  wearing vests and suits and jeans short size  
    
  On a regular busy day   
  one walked straight into a street pole  
  while another yelled and gestured to someone invisible around  
  one other sang aloud for no one particular troll  
  the zombie land had human beings not in control  
    
  In their artificial craving for privacy  
  the zombies enthusiastically dominate the digital river  
  socializing with other zombies near and far apart  
  they ignore the world nearby, living vicariously for ever

*A satirical view of the year 2020 and beyond*
