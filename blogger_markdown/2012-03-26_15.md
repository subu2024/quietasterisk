  Her eyes smiled back  
  Fleetingly acknowledging his presence in her life  
  Somewhere in the moment she wallowed  
  For there was no real love in there  
    
  They led the life as a couple  
  Participating diligently in the worldly matters  
  Hidden from the gaze of the outside  
  They did not share an ounce of love  
    
  The daily life continued  
  The dance kept the onlookers busy  
  The music had stopped long back  
  But the automatic rhythm kept the questions away  
    
  Desperate to save the remaining life  
  He sometimes bent backward to accommodate her  
  It lasted only for a brief time  
  Before the game was back at the same location  
    
  She cuddled and said sweet things  
  Shining with him, she wished no more  
  Ultimately the truth cannot be hidden forever  
  Her lies eventually surfaced in full bloom

  He wished for this to end  
  Without hurting anyone anymore  
  Far away was the goal post visible  
  Fifteen years was the time he had to pay the toll
